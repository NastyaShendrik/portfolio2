Nastya Shendrik Say Hi

Hi there!

This is a story about me as a person — not just as a professional.

I believe that one flows into the other. Our life principles shape the choices we make every minute in our work.

I didn’t become a designer because it was trendy. I decided to become one as a child — that’s why I went to art school. Whenever I saw something that made me uncomfortable, I tried to rethink it. Whether it was clothing, a layout, or the way something was done. And in all of this, one thing stood out — a love for essence and minimalism. Fewer details, thoughtful composition, flawless logic, and vivid emotion — these are my tools.

Once, when I was bored while visiting the countryside, my aunt told me that much depends on the person. Since then, I’ve taken matters into my own hands and looked for ways to take initiative. After all, if something doesn’t go according to plan — it’s a chance to try and change it.

It might seem like I’m always rushing somewhere — and yes, I love movement. But I always make time for the people I care about. I cherish moments of quiet, meaningful conversations, and love feeling life in the present.

I share my thoughts and notes from the life of a product designer in my Telegram channel.
